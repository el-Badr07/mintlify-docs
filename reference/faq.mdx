## General Questions

**Q: What is Intelligate?**
A: AI-powered claims management platform for manufacturing operations.

**Q: Who uses Intelligate?**
A: Employees report issues, managers oversee teams, admins configure system.

**Q: Does it replace email and tickets?**
A: Yes - unified claims, comments, notifications, SLA tracking in one place.

## Technical Questions

**Q: Can I login with my corporate credentials?**
A: Yes - via SSO/Keycloak. No separate passwords needed.

**Q: How does AI work?**
A: Analyzes text/voice/image to suggest category, priority, root cause.

**Q: Can I override AI classification?**
A: Yes - click to change. Your correction trains the AI.

**Q: How is my data secured?**
A: Keycloak authentication, role-based access, encrypted storage, audit logs.

## Workflow Questions

**Q: How do I create a claim?**
A: Click "New Claim", select text/voice/image, add details, submit.

**Q: What's an SLA?**
A: Service Level Agreement - deadline to resolve the claim.

**Q: What do status colors mean?**
A: Green=OK, Yellow=At-risk, Red=Overdue.

**Q: Can I assign to someone?**
A: Yes - click "Assign to" and select team member.

## Admin Questions

**Q: How do I add users?**
A: In Keycloak Admin Console, create user and assign roles.

**Q: How do I change SLA deadlines?**
A: Admin Console → Organization → SLA Policies.

**Q: Can I customize categories?**
A: Yes - Admin Console → Categories.
