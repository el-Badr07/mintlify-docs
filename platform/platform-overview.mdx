## What is Reclamia?

Reclamia is an AI-powered claims management platform designed for industrial, manufacturing, and other organizational operations. It streamlines how teams report, track, and resolve operational issues, safety incidents, logistics problems, maintenance requests, and other concerns.

### The Problem We Solve

Operational teams face constant challenges:
- Quality issues that need immediate documentation and resolution
- Safety incidents requiring rapid incident management and compliance
- Logistics issues causing supply chain disruptions
- Maintenance problems impacting system uptime
- Multiple systems and siloed information preventing collaboration

These issues often go unreported or documented inconsistently, leading to:
- Prolonged resolution times
- Repeated problems
- Compliance risks
- Lost operational insights

## How Reclamia Works

Reclamia unifies claims management across your entire organization with three core capabilities:

### 1. Intelligent Capture

**Multiple input methods for flexibility:**
- **Text claims**: Detailed written descriptions from any device
- **Voice claims**: Quick on-site recording with automatic transcription
- **Image claims**: Photo or screenshot upload with automatic text extraction

**Automatic AI classification:**
- Categories (Quality, Safety, Logistics, Maintenance)
- Priority levels (Low, Medium, High, Critical)
- Root cause prediction
- Automatic routing to appropriate departments

### 2. Real-Time Collaboration

**Team workflows that accelerate resolution:**
- Assign claims to team members instantly
- Add comments with @mentions for quick communication
- React with emojis for lightweight feedback
- View complete activity history and changes
- Attach evidence files and documentation
- Escalate claims when needed
- Track all changes in audit logs

### 3. Smart Automation & Insights

**SLA tracking & compliance:**
- Automatic deadline calculation based on priority
- Status indicators (On-time, At-risk, Overdue)
- Escalation warnings before breaches
- Compliance metrics dashboard

**Advanced analytics:**
- Dashboard with key performance indicators
- Team workload tracking by department
- Trend analysis over time
- Root cause analysis
- Custom reports and CSV/PDF export

**Intelligent assistance:**
- AI chat assistant for instant insights
- Query claims using natural language
- Automated suggestions and recommendations
- Knowledge base integration for context-aware responses

## Core Use Cases

### Quality Management
Report defects with photos, automatic categorization, and root cause tracking to prevent recurring issues.

### Safety Incident Management
Rapid incident documentation with automatic escalation based on severity, ensuring compliance and team notification.

### Logistics Support
Track shipment issues, supplier problems, and delivery delays with automated routing to logistics teams.

### Maintenance Scheduling
Equipment problems captured with photos and descriptions, automatically routed to maintenance specialists with historical context.

### Cross-Functional Coordination
Complex issues that span multiple departments with built-in escalation and collaboration workflows.

## Platform Architecture

Reclamia consists of three integrated layers:

### User-Facing Interface
Web-based platform accessible to all team members for creating and managing claims, with mobile-responsive design.

### AI Intelligence Layer
Automatic classification, transcription, OCR, and AI-powered chat assistant for intelligent insights.

### Enterprise Foundation
Enterprise authentication (SSO/Keycloak), role-based access control, data security, audit logging, and multi-organization support.
