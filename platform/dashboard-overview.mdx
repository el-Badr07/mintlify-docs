## Overview

The Dashboard is your central hub for monitoring claims, team performance, and organizational health. It provides real-time insights tailored to your role.

---

## Key Metrics

**Top-level KPIs displayed:**
- **Total Claims**: All active claims with trend indicator
- **Open Claims**: Awaiting assignment or action
- **In Progress**: Actively being worked
- **SLA Compliance Rate**: Percentage meeting deadlines (Green 90%+, Yellow 75-90%, Red below 75%)
- **Critical Claims**: High-urgency issues requiring immediate attention
- **Overdue Claims**: Past SLA deadline with escalation recommended
- **Average Resolution Time**: Mean time from creation to closure

---

## Team Workload

**Department View:**
- Claims per department with priority breakdown
- Identify resource bottlenecks
- See which teams need help

**Individual Workload:**
- Claims assigned to each team member
- SLA compliance per person
- Balance workload distribution
- Recognize top performers

---

## SLA Performance

**Status Overview:**
- **On-Time** (Green): Claims meeting deadlines
- **At-Risk** (Yellow): Approaching deadline in 2 hours
- **Overdue** (Red): Past deadline, escalation required

**Department Comparison:**
- SLA compliance by department
- Identify underperforming areas
- Focus improvement efforts

---

## Priority & Category Breakdown

**Priority Distribution:**
- Visual chart showing Critical, High, Medium, Low percentages
- Healthy distribution: Critical below 20%, most Medium/Low
- High Critical rate may indicate process issues

**Categories by Type:**
- Claims breakdown by category (Quality, Safety, Logistics, Maintenance, Other)
- Month-over-month trending
- Identify recurring problems
- Allocate resources accordingly

---

## Filters & Customization

**Filter by:**
- Date range (Today, Week, Month, Custom)

- Priority level

## Common Insights

**High claim volume?**
- May need additional staff
- Check for systemic issues
- Review process efficiency

**Low SLA compliance?**
- Review SLA settings for realism
- Check resource constraints
- Consider training needs

**Unbalanced workload?**
- Reassign claims to balance
- Review auto-assignment rules
- Adjust team allocation
