## Reports Overview

Export your claims data for analysis, compliance, and stakeholder sharing.

### Pre-Built Reports

**Department Performance Report**
- All claims in specific department
- SLA compliance metrics
- Team member breakdown
- Trend analysis
- Ready for PDF export

**Category Analysis Report**
- All claims in specific category
- Breakdown by priority
- Resolution time analysis
- Recurring issues highlighted
- Exportable for trend review

**SLA Compliance Report**
- All claims with SLA metrics
- On-time vs overdue breakdown
- Worst-performing categories
- Individual performance
- Compliance documentation

### Custom Date Filtering

1. **Select report type**
2. **Choose date range**: Last 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, custom
3. **Filter options**: Department, category, priority
4. **Generate report**

### Export Formats

**CSV Export**
- Open in Excel or Google Sheets
- All data included
- Ready for pivot tables
- For detailed analysis

**PDF Export**
- Professional formatting
- Branded with your logo
- Charts and summaries
- Ready to share

**Scheduled Reports**
- Set to run daily/weekly/monthly
- Automatically email to stakeholders
- Consistent tracking
- Save time

## Creating Reports

### Step 1: Choose Report Type
Select from pre-built or custom

### Step 2: Set Parameters
- Time period
- Department/Category filter
- Metrics to include

### Step 3: Generate
System creates report with visualizations

### Step 4: Export
Download CSV or PDF

## Department Breakdowns

Reports can be segmented by:
- Department (Production, Quality, etc.)
- Category (Quality, Safety, Logistics, Maintenance)
- Priority (Critical, High, Medium, Low)
- Team member
- Time period
