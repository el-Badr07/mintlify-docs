## Claims Management

### Multimodal Claim Creation
Create claims the way that works best for your situation:

- **Text Claims**: Detailed descriptions from your desk with full context
- **Voice Claims**: Quick on-site recording with automatic transcription via Groq Whisper
- **Image Claims**: Photo or screenshot with automatic text extraction via GPT-4 Vision

All input methods are automatically classified and routed to the appropriate team.

### Intelligent Routing & Classification
AI automatically processes every claim:

- **Category Assignment**: Quality, Safety, Logistics, Maintenance, or custom categories
- **Priority Determination**: Low, Medium, High, or Critical based on content analysis
- **Root Cause Prediction**: Automatically suggests likely causes from historical data
- **Department Routing**: Routes to the right team based on classification
- **Confidence Scoring**: Shows how certain the AI is about its decisions

### Claim Status Workflow
Track claims through their complete lifecycle:

- **OPEN**: Newly reported claims awaiting assignment
- **IN_PROGRESS**: Assigned to a team member and being worked on
- **RESOLVED**: Solution found, awaiting closure
- **CLOSED**: Final resolution confirmed and documented

## SLA & Deadline Management

### Automatic SLA Calculation
Reclamia calculates deadlines automatically:

- Response time targets based on priority
- Resolution time targets by category
- Department-specific SLA rules
- Custom SLA policies per organization

### Status Indicators
Visual SLA tracking at a glance:

- **On-Time**: Green status - claim is being handled within deadline
- **At-Risk**: Yellow warning - approaching deadline, escalation recommended
- **Overdue**: Red alert - deadline has passed, immediate action needed

### Escalation Management
Prevent SLA breaches:

- Automatic escalation warnings as deadline approaches
- Escalate to managers when needed
- Priority elevation for critical claims
- Breach prevention workflows

### SLA Analytics
Track your compliance:

- Organization-wide SLA compliance rate
- Department-level metrics
- Category-specific performance
- Trend analysis over time
