## AI in Reclamia

Reclamia uses AI for three core capabilities:

1. **Automatic Classification**: AI analyzes each claim and predicts category, priority, root cause
2. **Intelligent Chat Assistant**: Ask questions and get instant insights
3. **Knowledge Base Integration**: RAG service grounds AI responses in your documentation

## How AI Helps

**Speed**: Automatic classification saves manual categorization time
**Accuracy**: AI learns from historical data
**Insights**: Chat assistant answers questions instantly
**Learning**: Knowledge base prevents re-learning past solutions

## AI Capabilities

- **Text analysis**: Processes claim descriptions
- **Voice transcription**: Groq Whisper converts audio to text
- **Image analysis**: GPT-4 Vision extracts text from images
- **Root cause prediction**: Suggests likely causes
- **Sentiment analysis**: Detects urgency from wording
